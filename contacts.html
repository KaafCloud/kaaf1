What makes Kaaf Cloud different from other IT service providers?

Kaaf Cloud specializes exclusively in the Microsoft ecosystem. We are not a generic IT provider; we are your dedicated Microsoft Cloud Partner. This deep focus on Microsoft Azure, Microsoft 365, and related security and management services allows us to deliver unmatched expertise, proven frameworks, and a customer-centric approach to ensure your cloud journey directly aligns with your business outcomes.

Are you a certified Microsoft partner?

Yes, as a trusted Microsoft Cloud Partner, our team holds deep Microsoft certifications and specializations. We combine this official expertise with hands-on experience to design, deploy, and manage mission-critical environments. Our commitment is to leverage the full power of the Microsoft cloud platform for your organization's benefit.
How do you handle backup and disaster recovery?
We design and implement robust Backup, Disaster Recovery (DR), and Business Continuity plans tailored to your Microsoft cloud environment. This includes configuring automated backups for critical data in Azure and M365, defining clear Recovery Point (RPO) and Recovery Time (RTO) objectives, and conducting regular DR drills to ensure a swift and effective restoration of services in case of an outage or data loss.
How does Kaaf Cloud help secure our Microsoft cloud environment?
We take a layered security approach. Our Cloud Security & Compliance Services start with an assessment of your current posture. We then implement and manage critical controls: identity and access management (via Entra ID), data protection (DLP, encryption), threat prevention, and endpoint security (via Intune). We ensure your M365 and Azure configurations follow security best practices by default.
What are your SOC services, and do we need our own SOC?
Our SOC & Threat Management Services bring enterprise-grade security operations to your organization. You do not need to build your own costly SOC. We leverage tools like Microsoft Sentinel to provide real-time security monitoring, threat detection, incident response, and vulnerability management. Our experts investigate alerts, manage threats, and provide you with clear reporting, significantly enhancing your security posture.

We want to migrate to Microsoft 365. How does Kaaf Cloud manage this process?
We execute Microsoft 365 migrations with a focus on minimal risk and disruption. Our process includes thorough planning and tenant governance design, followed by seamless migration of mailboxes (Exchange), data (OneDrive/SharePoint), and collaboration tools (Teams). We manage the technical execution, user communication, and post-migration support to ensure a smooth transition.
